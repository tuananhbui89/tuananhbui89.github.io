Erasing Concepts - Experimental Results 2

Experiments

Erasing ImageNet concepts

In the previous experiment, we erase artist concept Kelly Mckernan from the Stable Diffusion pretrained model. However, recognizing the success or failure of the erasing process is quite subjective. Therefore, we also try to erase ImageNet concepts which is easier to make assessment (i.e., by using a pretrained classifier). More specifically, we choose the Imagenette which is 10 easily classified classes from Imagenet ('Cassette Player', 'Chain Saw', 'Church', 'Gas Pump', 'Tench', 'Garbage Truck', 'English Springer', 'Golf Ball', 'Parachute', 'French Horn').

  • Erased concepts: Cassette Player, Church, Garbage Truck, Parachute, French Horn
  • Preserved concepts: Tench, English Springer, Chain Saw, Gas Pump, Golf Ball
