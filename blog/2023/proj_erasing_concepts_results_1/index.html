Erasing Concepts - Experimental Results

Experiments

Erasing artist concept

We reproduce the experiment to erase artist concept call Kelly Mckernan (you can find Kelly Mckernan’s artworks here). The reason is that Kelly Mckernan’s artworks are quite popular (Kelly Mckernan is one of 100 Most Infuential People in AI) and very likely be used to train large generative models such as Stable Diffusion, Midjourney. Therefore, “Kelly Mckernan” is a quite common target concept to be erased as in several previous works .

To make sure that the erasing method doesn’t affect/neglect other concepts, we also generate images with concepts from famous artists such as The Starry Night by Vincent van Gogh or Vincent’s Bedroom in Arles by Vincent van Gogh. Erasing ImageNet concepts

In the previous experiment, we erase artist concept Kelly Mckernan from the Stable Diffusion pretrained model. However, recognizing the success or failure of the erasing process is quite subjective. Therefore, we also try to erase ImageNet concepts which is easier to make assessment (i.e., by using a pretrained classifier). More specifically, we choose the Imagenette which is 10 easily classified classes from Imagenet ('Cassette Player', 'Chain Saw', 'Church', 'Gas Pump', 'Tench', 'Garbage Truck', 'English Springer', 'Golf Ball', 'Parachute', 'French Horn').

  • Erased concepts: Cassette Player, Church, Garbage Truck, Parachute, French Horn
  • Preserved concepts: Tench, English Springer, Chain Saw, Gas Pump, Golf Ball
